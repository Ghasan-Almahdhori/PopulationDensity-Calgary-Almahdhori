Title: 2019 Calgary Community PopulationCreator: Ghassan Saleh AlmahdhoriPublisher/Owner: The City of CalgarySource: 2019 Calgary Community Census, City of Calgary Open Data PortalService URL: https://services2.arcgis.com/XSv3KNGfmrd1txPN/arcgis/rest/services/2019_Census_by_Community_Calgary/FeatureServer Dataset language/encoding: eng / UTF-8Abstract:Calgary community population calculated from 2019 census data downloaded from the City of Calgary Open Data website. The dataset represents the total population within official community boundaries as of 2019. It was used to calculate population density (population per square kilometre) by dividing population counts by corresponding community areas.Purpose:To quantify community-level population for analysis of urban density and sustainability indicators in Calgary.Temporal/Update:Reference year: 2019Update frequency: Every municipal census (typically every 2–3 years)Date accessed: 2025-11-02Spatial:Extent (approximate): W: -114.3158, E: -113.8599, N: 51.2124, S: 50.8428Spatial representation: Vector (community polygons)CRS: NAD 1983 CSRS Alberta 10-TM (EPSG:3403)Suggested Fields:  community_id – unique community identifier    community_name – official name of community    population_total – total number of residents    area_km2 – land area of community in square kilometres    pop_density – population per km² (derived field in project)Processing (project-level):Population density calculated in ArcGIS Online using the “2019 Calgary Community Population” layer joined to community boundary polygons. Attribute field created by dividing total population by land area.Constraints / Use Notes:None; dataset is public and unrestricted.
