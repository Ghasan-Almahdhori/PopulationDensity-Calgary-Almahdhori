Dataset title: 2021 Federal Census – Mode of Transportation to Work by CommunitySource: City of Calgary Open Data (Statistics Canada 2021)ArcGIS Online layer:  https://services1.arcgis.com/AVP60cs0Q9PEA8rH/arcgis/rest/services/2021%20Federal%20Census%20Transportation%20to%20Work%20by%20Community/FeatureServer Access method: Public read-onlyDescription:Transportation to Work Statistics from the 2021 Census of Canada. The data was provided by Statistics Canada, acquired by Community Strategies, and compiled by The City of Calgary.Mode of transportation to work refers to the main mode of transportation a person uses to travel between their home and their place of work. Persons who used more than one mode of transportation were asked to identify the single mode they used for most of the travel distance.This dataset does not measure multiple modes of transportation, nor seasonal variation, nor trips for purposes other than commuting.This is a one-time load of Statistics Canada federal census data from 2021 applied to the Communities, Wards, and City boundaries current as of 2022 (so they may not match newer boundaries).Update frequency: Every 5 years.Data Steward: Community Strategies (Demographics and Evaluation).Usage: For general public and internal City business groups.Notes:Data export not permitted; the above link provides reproducibility and citation compliance for this dataset.
